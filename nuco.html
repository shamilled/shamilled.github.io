Another organelle inside of the nucleus!

  • Inside of the nucleus is a concentration of chromosomes surrounded by a double membrane known as the Nucleolus.

  • The Nucleolus is where rRNA and ribosomes are made.

  • These ribosomes then leave the Nucleolus and the nucleus to go make proteins for the cell.
