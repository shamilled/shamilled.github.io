Weight vs. Mass

Mass is the quantity of matter in an object.

All you need is matter to have mass.

Your mass is the same here on earth as it is on the moon or anywhere else in space.

Weight is a combination of mass and gravity working together.

Weight is the downward force of an object due to gravity.

You weigh more on earth than you would on the moon because the moon has less gravity.

Free falling objects are weightless but still have the same mass.

    Have you ever heard of astronauts experiencing Weightlessness when they are in space?

  • Astronauts don't feel weight in space because they don't feel gravity while they are in space.

  • HOWEVER, they still have the same Mass in space as they do on earth. Astronauts are not massless in space.

Fun Fact: Did you know that when the satellites and space stations are orbiting earth they are actually in free fall around the earth! This is why they are weightless.
