How does the cell know what to do?

  • The Nucleus is the command center of the cell. You can think of it as the "brain" or the "boss" of the cell.
  • It holds the genetic information (DNA) that tells the cells what to do.
  • The instructions held in the Nucleus tell the cell:
    • How to synthesize (build) proteins
    • Which proteins to make
    • How to reproduce (make more cells)
