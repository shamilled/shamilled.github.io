Converting Temperature




Convert the temperatures from one temperature scale to the other.

Fahrenheit to Celsius:

  1. Subtract 32° from the Fahrenheit temperature
  2. Then multiply the result from Step 1 by 5
  3. Then divide the result from Step 2 by 9

(°F - 32) × 5 ÷ 9

Ex: (68 °F - 32) × 5 ÷ 9 = 20 °C

Celsius to Fahrenheit:

  1. Multiply Celsius temperature by 9
  2. Then divide the result from Step 1 by 5
  3. Then add 32° to the result from Step 2

°C × 9 ÷ 5 + 32°

Ex: 20 °C × 9 ÷ 5 + 32° = 68 °F
