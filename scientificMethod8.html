Procedures

Procedures are the directions for an experiment.

You should write down your procedure in a way that anyone can follow along and do your experiment, too.

Procedures should include:

  • Measurements for all of the materials you used
  • The unit (gram, liter, centimeter, etc.) at the end of a number
  • When to make measurements and record the data
  • How much time to wait for certain parts of the experiment
  • List all the controls
  • Describe how to change the independent variable
  • Describe how to measure the dependent variable
  • List any tools that will be used
