There are four major types of Blood Cells:

  • Red Blood Cells
  • White Blood Cells
  • Platelets
  • Plasma Cells

The different Blood Cells allow your blood to accomplish many different tasks. These include:

  • Transport nutrients and oxygen to tissues and the lungs
  • Form clots to prevent excess bleeding from cuts or scrapes
  • Help fight infections
  • Take waste to kidneys and liver to keep the blood and body clean
