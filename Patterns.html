"Virtual World of Patterns"takes Dr. Laubenbacher's Interactive Session on Patterns and creates a fun environment for kids to learn. Through your participation, this virtual lab will be updated regularly. This will allow the virtual lab to grow along with you! This lab will not only help you to learn anout fractals and patterns, but it will also teach you how to have fun with math!

This website offers:
  • Interactive learning tools that are enjoyable to students ages 9-12, where they have the opportunity to ask questions, give answers, and find out more about patterns
  • Mathematics activities can be found that encourage students to work together while learning new things. This pushes students to become Pattern Hunters in their own lives!!

Follow the links to the right to investigate more of the patterns found around us! Be sure to scroll down on the links to see the full list we have to offer!
