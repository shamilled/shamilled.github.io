Volume of Containers

    Gases expand to fill up the whole volume of the container that they are in.


  • To find the volume of the gas, we need to find the volume of the container that it is in.

  • Containers come in many shapes and sizes. We are going to focus on two main shapes:

    1. Rectangular Prisms
      (note: cubes are a special type of rectangular prism where all sides are the same length)

    2. Cylinders
