Design the Experiment: Variables

To make your Experiment you need to figure out what all can affect your outcome.

In other words, you need to figure out what the Variables are. A variable is something that changes (varies) or can change. Our variables are:

  • Type of Fertilizer
  • The day you measure height
  • Sunlight
  • Planting Pot
  • Water
  • Height of the plants
  • Soil
  • Type of Seeds
  • Ruler

There are two types of variables:

  • Independent Variable: Does not depend on the other variables. This is the variable that you control.
  • Dependent Variable: Depends on the other variables. It is the variable that you are trying to measure.

You can see that some of the variables we listed we are not trying to test. We only want to see how different fertilizers affect plant height. This is why we control the variables that we are not testing.
