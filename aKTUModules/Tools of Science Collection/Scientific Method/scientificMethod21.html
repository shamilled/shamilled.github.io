Pie Charts

  • Pie Charts help us see fractions of a whole. Percentages and fractions help us predict the chance of something happening.

  • For example, we decide to do the plant experiment three more times and record the final heights of each plant in the table on the right. Now we want to see what fraction of the plants grew over 20 cm for each fertilizer.

  • Fertilizer 1 only had 1 in 4 plants grow over 20 cm.
    1/4 is 25%.

  • Fertilizer 2 had 2 in 4 plants grow over 20 cm.
    2/4 is 50%.
