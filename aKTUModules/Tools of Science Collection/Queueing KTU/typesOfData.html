Qualitative vs.Quantitative Measurements

This glass feels warmer than the other glass.



Correct!

This is qualitative because we are not measuring the temperatures.

That rock was 4 pounds heavier than the first.



Correct!

Pounds are a quantity of weight.

It is 76 degrees Fahrenheit in here.



Correct!

Degrees Fahrenheit are a quantity of temperature.

That concert took a long time.



Correct!

We did not say how long it took so we did not quantify it. So this is qualitative.

This fertilizer made the plants grow taller.



Correct!

We did not measure how much taller. This is a qualitative observation.

This fertilizer made the plants grow an average of 6 inches taller.



Correct!

Inches is a quantity of length. We measured the difference in heights and quantified it with inches.
