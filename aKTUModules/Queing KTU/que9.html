Data

In this instance, your data is Qualitative. That means you don't have numbers to work with. Instead, you have observed your store and noticed that you are getting backed up lines.

Qualitative Data: Deals with descriptions and does not use measurements. (Colors, smells, properties)

Quantitative Data: This data is measured quantities and involves numbers and units. (3 miles, 12 seconds, 8 kilograms)

Practice Identifying Qualitative vs. Quantitative Data before you continue.
