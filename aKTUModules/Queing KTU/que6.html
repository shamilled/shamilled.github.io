Hypothesis

In your research, you studied traffic at your store and found that 15 people came into your store per hour. You also found out that one cashier can serve 5 people per hour.

You form the following Hypothesis: If I hire enough cashiers to check out 15 people per hour then the lines in my store will not back up because I will have just enough cashiers to balance the demand of the customers.

So 3 cashiers should be the right amount to hire for the store.
