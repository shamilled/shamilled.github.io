Displacement

    Displacement helps us find the volume of a solid object.


  • To find this volume, we fill a graduated cylinder with enough water to cover the object.
    (we'll use 50 mL)

  • Then, we place an object in the graduated cylinder and the water level rises. In our case the water level rises to 150 mL.

  • This means that our object has a volume of 100 mL. We calculate this by subtracting the initial volume from the final volume.
