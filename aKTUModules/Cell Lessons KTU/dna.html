What is DNA?

  • DNA is short for Deoxyribonucleic Acid. This holds all of the organism's genetic information.
  • The genetic information tells the cell what proteins to make, how many of them, and when to make them. This ultimately leads to the expression of traits.
  • DNA is like a twisted ladder. The 'rungs' of the ladder are what holds the genetic information in a very specific sequence of Base Pairs.
