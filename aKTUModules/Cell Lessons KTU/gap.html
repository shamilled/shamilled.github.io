What is the Golgi Apparatus?

  • The Golgi Apparatus is composed of stacks of flattened membrane sacs.
  • The Golgi Apparatus receives lipids (fats) and proteins from other organelles. It performs finishing touches with these lipids and proteins such as:
    • Modifying
    • Sorting
    • Packaging
  • From here, lipids and proteins are transported to different parts of the cell using vesicles. The Golgi Apparatus is kind of like the "UPS" of the cell.
