What do Lysosomes do?

  • Lysosomes are sacs that contain digestive enzymes.

  • They use the enzymes to break down old organelles and other substances that the cell regards as "trash" into parts that the cell can use again or get rid of.

  • Lysosomes are made in the golgi apparatus.
