The genetic information found inside the nucleus is stored in the form of Chromosomes.

  • Chromosomes are composed of Proteins, DNA, and RNA.
  • Each Chromosome is a lot of DNA bunched up tightly with proteins that help pack it together.
  • The Chromosomal Theory of Inheritance states that hereditary traits are carried in chromosomes. In other words, this is where your cells store your genes.
