  • Nerve Cells, or Neurons, are the communication network between the brain and the rest of the body.
  • When a Nerve Cell is stimulated (by touch, heat, light) the Dendrites send a signal through the Axon which is then sent either to other nerves, muscles, or glands.
  • For example, if you touch a hot surface, the Nerve Cells in your hand send signals to your brain telling it that your arm muscles need to contract and pull your hand away from danger.
