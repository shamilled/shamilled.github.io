Analyze the Data

  • When you analyze your data, you are trying to make sense of it and learn from it. You should put your data into charts and graphs to help you view your data better.

  • Charts and graphs help make the data from your table more visual. This will help you spot trends and reach conclusions.

  • One great way to visualize data that you want to compare, like heights or amounts, is with Bar Graphs.
