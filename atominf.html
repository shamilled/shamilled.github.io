Just like cells are the building blocks of living organisms, Atoms are the building blocks of all of matter.

  • Atoms have even smaller pieces (subatomic particles) with different charges:
    • Protons: carry a positive charge (+)
    • Neutrons: carry no charge (neutral)
    • Electrons: carry a negative charge (-)

  • The Atom has a nucleus (center), which is where the protons and neutrons are found. The nucleus is surrounded by a "cloud" of electrons that orbit the nucleus.

  • The number of protons an Atom has determines what element that Atom is. For example, all Oxygen Atoms have 8 protons while all Helium Atoms have 2 protons.
