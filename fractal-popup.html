Would you like to send us your fractal image to be displayed on the website? Get help from your parents or guardians, and just follow the steps below!

Windows Users
1- With your fractal image visible on the screen, press the "Print screen" button found directly on your keyboard (often abbreviated Prt Scr, Print Scrn, PrtScn, Prt Sc, Prt Scrn, or Prnt Scrn) Usually found on the right-hand side of the keyboard
2- After you press your "Print screen" button, open Windows Paint (Start Menu, All Programs, Accessories, Paint)
3- Once Paint has opened, press "Paste"
4- A screenshot of your internet browser (containing a picture of your Fractal art) should appear in Paint.
5- Next, "Save as" a .jpg or.jpeg image, to your desktop
6- Find the image on your desktop, and send the image to kbrewer@vbi.vt.edu as an email attachment.

Mac Users
1- With your fractal image visible on the screen, press "command-shift-3"
2- A screenshot of your browser window will be saved to your desktop as an image file
3- Send the image file to kbrewer@vbi.vt.edu as an email attachment.
