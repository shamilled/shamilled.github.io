Cell

Cells are the smallest structural and functional units of living organisms. They have many jobs, such as making proteins and fighting off diseases.

Tissues

A group of cells with similar jobs combine to form tissues. Tissues have specific tasks such as digestionStomach Lining, movementMuscle Tissue
, and supportBones
.

Organs

When a group of similar tissues work together to complete a common task, they are called organs. Organs can also work together in systems to help get many different jobs done.

Organ Systems

When organs work together to do a large function like digesting food or circulating blood, they are working together in an organ system.

Organisms

Organisms like bugs, animals, and plants are a collection of different organs working together to make life possible!
